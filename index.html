The City Of Sheffield

Sheffield is city that was founded on the 10th of August 1297. It is located in South Yorkshire, England.
The city derives its name from the River Sheaf which runs through the city.

Mini Descriptions of Tourist Attractions.

  • The University of Sheffield:
    A public research univeristy that was founded in 1905, it is a member of the Russell-Group research intesive universities and it has been ranked as one of the top 100 universities by QS for the last 15 years. Read more on T.U.O.S.

  • Kelham Island Museum:
    Kelham museum is located on a man-made island which resulted from the construction of a mill race in the 12th century it was opened in 1982.
    Read more here K.I.M.

  • Weston Park Museum:
    Weston park is one of the oldest tourist attractions in the city, it was founded in 1875, and attracts about 250,000 peole yearly. it was nominated for the presitgous Gulbenkian prize in 2007.
    Read more at Weston.

  • Graves Art Gallery:
    Graves art is on the premier art galleries in England, it houses permanent displays from the city's historic and contemporary collection of British and European art, it was open in 1934 and located in Central Library, Sheffield City Centre. Read more at GAG.

  • Elsecar Heritage Railway:

    • EHR was established in 1850 to serve collieries and iron works, and closed in 1984. The Barnsley council started a restoration project in 1994 and reopned the line as a heritage railway/Tourist attraction in 1996 . Read more at EHR.
